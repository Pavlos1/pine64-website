## Pinenut Model-12S Wi-Fi/BLE5 Stamp

The **Pinenut-12S** module is a Wi-Fi and BLE5 Stamp.

Unlike the Pinenut-01S, the Pinenut-12S is castellated, allowing it to be directly soldered onto a carrier board.
